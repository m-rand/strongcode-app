# Naming Ideas for StrongCode

## Current Name
**StrongCode** / **strong.code**

---

## Brand Architecture: ".code" Family

### Umbrella Terms (covering all products)
| Name | Notes |
|------|-------|
| **body.code** | Most universal - covers strength, muscle, performance, longevity |
| **train.code** | Direct, action-oriented |
| **adapt.code** | Captures the dynamic/adaptive programming nature |
| **iron.code** | Iron is universal (barbells, kettlebells) |
| **human.code** | Human performance/optimization |
| **phase.code** | Periodization is the core concept |

### Product Names by Focus Area

| Focus | Primary | Alternatives |
|-------|---------|--------------|
| **Strength/Powerlifting** | strong.code | iron.code, power.code |
| **Hypertrophy** | mass.code | size.code, grow.code, build.code |
| **Weightlifting (Olympic)** | launch.code | speed.code, flight.code, oly.code |
| **Longevity** | long.code | life.code, last.code, vital.code, prime.code |
| **Kettlebells** | bell.code | swing.code |

### Proposed Family Structure
```
body.code (umbrella)
├── strong.code (strength)
├── mass.code (hypertrophy)
├── launch.code (weightlifting)
└── long.code (longevity)
```

## Alternative Naming Directions

### With "X" Suffix (SpaceX style)
- StrongX
- IronX
- LiftX
- ForceX
- PhaseX

### With "K" instead of "C" (Linn Audio style: Klimax, Majik, Selekt)
- **StrongKode** - StrongCode but with harder K
- IronKraft
- PhaseKraft
- BarKraft

### "-craft" Names (spacecraft + craftsmanship)
- **Phasecraft** - phases of periodization + craft
- **Barcraft** - Starcraft/Warcraft vibes for barbell
- **Liftcraft**
- **Ironcraft**

### Two-Word Memorable Names

**Investment/Long-term (good for longevity angle):**
- **Iron Dividend** - training as investment that pays dividends
- **Compound Iron** - compound interest of strength
- **Patient Iron** - long-term approach

**Math/Logic:**
- **Iron Math** / **Heavy Math** - calculated approach
- **Lift Logic** - logical lifting
- **Iron Equation**
- **Body Calculus**

**Systematic:**
- **Iron Protocol**
- **Load Blueprint**

**Poetic/Unique:**
- **Quiet Strength**
- **Earned Iron**
- **Slow Iron** (longevity)

### Waves/Oscillation (captures dynamic programming)
- **Iron Tide** - ebb and flow of volume
- **Swell** - wave + growth
- **Pendulum**

### Physics/Mechanics
- **Torque** - rotational force
- **Momentum** - building force
- **Iron Arc** - trajectory

### Sound/Feel
- **Clang** - sound of iron
- **Grind** - the process

---

## Distinctive Branding Elements (like Linn's K for C)

Possible consistent elements:
- Always use "K" instead of "C": StrongKode, IronKraft
- Always lowercase: strongcode
- Always with dot: strong.code
- Consistent suffix: -X, -Kode, -Kraft
- Nordic/Germanic feel: Strøng, Irön

---

## Notes

- Current system handles both **strength and hypertrophy** (adjustable parameters)
- Future expansion: weightlifting, kettlebells, longevity
- Key differentiator: **dynamic/adaptive programming** based on Plan Strong methodology
- The ".code" suffix works well - implies programming, algorithms, systematic approach
